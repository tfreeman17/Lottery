Lottery Game

Pick 4 distinct numbers between 1-10

Rules

1.You will start off with $10.
2. If you loose all your money you mush refresh to start over. You cannot go into debit.
3.You must pick 4 different numbers, but you are allowed to play the same numbers on different turns.

pick a number
pick a different number from the previous
pick a different number from the 2 previous
pick a different number from the 3 previous

Winnings

You win $4 if you match 2 numbers of the 4 numbers
$8 if you match three of the 4 numbers
$40 if they match all 4 numbers
